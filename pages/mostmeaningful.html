Most Meaningful Learning Objective

Directions

Choose the learning objective that you feel was most meaningful to your internship experience. Write a professional report describing how you benefited from the learning objective.
 
The first paragraph should simply copy the learning objective from your internship application.
 
In subsequent paragraphs:
-describe the activities you performed related to the learning objective,
-describe
what you learned as a result of these activities,
-conclude with a reflection on the effectiveness of the learning experience.
 
Your report should have a cover page, be 1000 words at a minimum, and single spaced. Be sure to proof read your paper before submitting. Use spelling and grammar checking.
